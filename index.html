Common questions about Calculate

Are the calculators on Calculate really free?

Yes — every calculator is completely free with no ads, no accounts and no locked features. The site is supported by optional donations only.

How accurate are the results?

Every tool is verified against known benchmarks before publishing — loan math against standard amortization tables, inflation against official BLS CPI data, health formulas against their original published equations. Where a result is an estimate, the page says so plainly.

Is my data private?

Completely. Calculations run entirely in your browser using JavaScript — the numbers you type never leave your device, and the site uses no cookies or tracking.

Do the calculators work on mobile?

Yes — every page is built for phones first and works instantly in any modern browser, with no app or sign-up required.
